As per the Paris Agreement, reducing the amount of carbon dioxide emissions is essential to meet global temperature standards, and thereby, efforts made to decarbonise have been put in place by various countries, with more than 150 governments having submitted plans to reduce carbon emissions by 2030. We strive to achieve the same using our 3-step approach.




Our 3-Step Approach

Here, at Decarb Expert, we offer consultancy services that are useful for different industries, be it Transportation, Power generation, Hospitality, HVAC, Construction etc. We follow a 3-step approach to help businesses reduce their Carbon Footprint:

DUE DILIGENCE
We’ll make a preliminary assessment of your baseline carbon footprint, based on the nature of your business activities. This can include things such as energy consumption (electrical, natural gas, etc.) or business travel, production or service activities performed by your suppliers, Employee commuter traffic etc. The Assessment will help us working on focus areas to bring down the same.



TARGET SETTING
Based on the above inputs, we will guide you to determine which target area to aim, and by when, to bring down your Carbon Footprint, in the most feasible manner. For instance, Powering the company’s operations with 100% renewable electricity, Reducing resource consumption in production processes, switching to electric vehicles for employee commuting, Considering the frequency and necessity of business travel, using energy efficient Heating, Ventilation and Air-conditioning systems etc.



EXECUTION & MONITORING
Once the target is set, execution needs to kick in. We will assist you in the implementation of the proposed solutions and achievement of your target, simultaneously trackin and working on performance report of the goals.
